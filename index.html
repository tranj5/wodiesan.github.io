"Specialization is for insects."
-Lazarus Long (as written by Robert Heinlen, Time Enough for Love)

In Time Enough for Love, Lazarus Long gives a hefty list of requirements in order for a person to be considered a competent man. I've been unknowingly living my life trying to bring myself closer to something similar to the way Heinlen envisions his characters. Majoring in an engineering field (or electronic engineering tech, if you want specifics) allows me to bring myself a few steps closer towards that direction.

Feasibility of Pulse Oximetry Implementation Through FPGA Technology

During the Spring 2014 semester of my sophomore year, myself and two other students proposed a research study to Wentworth's Department of Electrical Engineering and Technology. Due to an interesting turn of events, this independent research has been funded by the Dean of the College of Engineering and Technology. Our team is currently working towards a poster presentation of our work for the 2014 Polytechnic Summit. The far goal is to publish it within a related IEEE journal.
